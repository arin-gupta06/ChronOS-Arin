# CareerWiseAI

## 📋 Project Description
CareerWiseAI is an intelligent career guidance and development platform that leverages artificial intelligence to provide personalized career recommendations, skill assessments, and professional growth pathways. The platform helps users make informed decisions about their career trajectories and development opportunities.

## ✨ Features
- **Personalized Career Recommendations** - AI-driven suggestions based on skills, interests, and market trends
- **Skill Assessment** - Comprehensive evaluation of current competencies and identification of skill gaps
- **Career Path Planning** - Customized roadmaps for professional growth and advancement
- **Market Insights** - Real-time data on industry trends and job market demands
- **Professional Development Resources** - Curated learning materials and training recommendations
- **Resume Analysis** - AI-powered feedback on resume optimization
- **Job Matching** - Intelligent matching between user profiles and job opportunities
